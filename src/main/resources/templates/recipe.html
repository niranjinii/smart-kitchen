Spicy Garlic Butter Pasta

John Doe • 4.9 (120 reviews)

This spicy garlic butter pasta is the ultimate comfort food. Ready in just 25 minutes, it combines rich butter, fiery red pepper flakes, and plenty of parmesan for a restaurant-quality meal at home. Perfect for busy weeknights!

Total Time 25 mins
Yield 2 Servings

Ingredients

2 Servings
  • 250g Fettuccine pasta
  • 30g Unsalted butter
    Can be swapped with 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
    Can be swapped with Nutritional Yeast (Vegan)
